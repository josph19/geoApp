About Charrouk Geometry Explorer

Charrouk Geometry Explorer is an interactive web application designed by Sara Charrouk to help users visualize and explore a variety of 3D and 2D geometric shapes. From spheres and cubes to circles and triangles, this tool offers an immersive experience for understanding geometric concepts and properties.

Features

Users can click on any shape to learn more about its properties and mathematical significance. This makes learning geometry interactive, engaging, and fun.

Mission

My mission is to provide a comprehensive, easy-to-use learning platform for students and geometry enthusiasts. This app is built to enhance the learning process with a visually appealing and hands-on experience.

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Sara Charrouk, the creator of Charrouk Geometry Explorer. I designed this application to make geometry more approachable and visually exciting for learners of all ages. I hope you enjoy exploring as much as I enjoyed creating it!
